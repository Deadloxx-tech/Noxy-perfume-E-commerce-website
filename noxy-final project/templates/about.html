Our Story

Founded in 2020 with a vision to revolutionize the fragrance industry, NOXY emerged from a deep passion for the art of perfumery. Our journey began in a small atelier where master perfumers experimented with rare essences and timeless techniques.

Today, we continue to honor that tradition while embracing innovation, creating fragrances that tell stories, evoke emotions, and capture the essence of modern luxury.

Our Craft

Every NOXY fragrance is a masterpiece of olfactory art. We source the finest ingredients from around the world - from Bulgarian rose petals to Indonesian sandalwood, from French lavender to Indian jasmine.
